Getting Started

1

Create a Game

Set up a game and choose what kind of scores to track. Each game can have its own scoring system.

2

Add Players

Add players by name or scan QR codes for quick check-in. Players persist across all your events.

3

Build a Roster

Create a roster to group players for a specific event or tournament. Assign players from your list.

4

Record Scores

Enter scores as players compete. The leaderboard updates in real time.

5

Display the Leaderboard

Open a display window and project it on a second screen. Pick a theme that matches your event.

6

Customize the Theme

Switch between built-in themes or tweak colors to match your venue's aesthetic.
